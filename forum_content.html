How to setup Photoshop with your Student ID

Posted by Christine Hakim 1 hour ago

  1. This is a comprehensive steps to setup Photoshop with Student ID :
    1. Obtain a valid student ID from your educational institution.
    2. Visit the Adobe website and search for the Photoshop Student and Teacher Edition.
    3. Click on the "Buy now" button and follow the prompts to create an Adobe account or sign in to an existing one.
    4. Enter your student ID information when prompted during the purchase process.
    5. Once your purchase is complete, download and install the Photoshop software on your computer.
  2. It is important to ensure that your educational institution is an eligible institution that qualifies for Adobe's student pricing.
